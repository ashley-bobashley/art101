Making a PB&J:

  1. Acquire materials: Pre-cut bread, peanut butter, jelly, knife, napkin.
  2. Lay two pieces of bread down flat.
  3. Use knife to scoop up peanut butter.
  4. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  5. Wipe off knife.
  6. Use knife to scoop up jelly.
  7. Spread jelly on the other piece of bread.
  8. Press pieces of bread together.
  9. Cut sandwich in half.
